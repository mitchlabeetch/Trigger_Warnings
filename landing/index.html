Advanced Local Processing

Unlike other extensions that rely on cloud servers, Trigger Warnings uses a highly optimized local algorithm.

  • Frame Analysis: We sample video frames 60 times per second to detect visual patterns associated with specific triggers (like flashing lights or spiders).
  • Audio Fingerprinting: Our engine listens for specific frequency signatures (like screams or explosions) without recording any speech.
  • No Data Leaks: Since everything runs on your computer, your viewing history remains strictly private.
